Sorting Algothrim Runtimes

Purpose:

The goal of this experiment is to measure and compare the efficiency of sorting algorithms for sorting n elements. The algorithms that are examined are Insertion Sort, Quicksort, Quicksort with Insertion Sort, and Merge Sort.

Hypothesis:

Quicksort is more efficient than insertion sort for sorting large arrays. The asymptotic time complexity, or big-O, of insertion sort is n2 while the big-O of quicksort is nlog(n). However, when implementing each sorting algorithm, quicksort has a greater overhead than insertion sort and is less efficient than insertion sort for sorting smaller arrays.
